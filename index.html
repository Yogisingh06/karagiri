    About Karagiri

    Karagiri: The beautiful world of Indian Ethnic Wear

    Karagiri is one of the leading trendsetters in the world of Indian ethnic wear. We have beautiful collections of Sarees,Lehenga Cholis, Anarkalis and more and we believe in supporting weavers around the country to present their art to the world with the help of Karagiri. The amazing collections showcase the beautiful craftsmanship of artisans around the country with different styles and designs. The different patterns represent the differences in cultures beautifully and tell a story about the region and its artists. Online sarees have become a sensation again with beautiful designs and with designers bringing fresh and contemporary ideas to fuse with traditional designs.


    We at Karagiri have a mix of traditional and trendy; which means there is something for everyone on Karagiri. Women's wardrobes in India are incomplete without the beautiful online sarees from Karagiri as the sarees are vibrant and beautifully crafted to perfection just for you.




    Choosing the right Saree

    With so many choices in sarees online, it is important to choose the right saree for you. Different kinds of beautiful sarees are worn on special occasions and festivals around the country. The sarees loved by women across the country and around the world include:


    Banarasi Sarees: Banarasi sarees are the most popular and well-loved sarees across the globe. The elegance and charm of these sarees come from a perfect mix of golden zari with vibrant colours that make the sarees elegant. Karagiri has a beautiful collection of banarasi sarees online that will make you look like a stunner.


    Kanjivaram Sarees: Kanjivaram Sarees are the traditional sarees from the southern part of the country. These sarees have a gold shimmer because of the amazingly detailed Kanchipuram design in golden zari along with beautiful bright colours. You can choose from a wide range of beautiful kanjivaram sarees in vibrant colours on Karagiri!


    Paithani Sarees: Paithani sarees are beautiful sarees that come from the state of Maharashtra. These sarees are worn by women for weddings and important festivals. These sarees are heirlooms passed down from generation to generation. These beautiful sarees are bright as they have broad golden zari borders with colourful motifs of birds and nature. The sarees online are now loved by women across the world for their vibrant colours and beautiful designs. Karagiri offers a wide range of paithani sarees at affordable prices that will make you look and feel confident, perfect for any occasion.
